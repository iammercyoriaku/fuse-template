Custom Directives
ms-form-wizard
Description:

ms-form-wizard directive is a helper directive that will help you to create form wizards. Combined with md-tabs and form elements, you will be able to create as much as steps you want.

Example: 1 Personal Information
Firstname field is required.
2 Address
Address field is required.
Address can't be longer than 150 characters.
3 Payment Method
Card holder field is required.
